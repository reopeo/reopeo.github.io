title: Autonomous Slope Detection and Guidance
summary: Detecting ramps with 3D LiDAR and RGB imagery for wheelchair-type mobile robots.
tags:

---

This system detects a slope entrance from a distance, navigates a
wheelchair-type robot to it, and performs the climb. It was developed and
validated on a real robot as an undergraduate research project.
